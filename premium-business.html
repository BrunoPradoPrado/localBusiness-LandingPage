Our Philosophy

Where Precision

Dr. Claire Fontaine founded Lumière with a simple belief: dental care should never feel rushed, cold, or clinical. Every patient who walks through our door deserves unhurried attention, transparent communication, and results that last.

Our practice combines the latest advancements in dental technology with a warmth that puts even the most anxious patients at ease. We treat the whole person — not just the tooth.
